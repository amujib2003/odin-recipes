Pizza

Description

This is a classic dish you can't miss out on. Pizza has always been a staple and you can never go wrong with it. It feeds the whole family and it isn't too big of a time commitment. In total, with this recipe it has 6 servings and it only takes 1 hour and 33 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup warm water (100° to 110°)
  • 2 teaspoons granulated sugar
  • 1 package dry yeast (about 2 1/4 teaspoons)
  • 3.65 ounces white rice flour (about 3/4 cup)
  • 1.4 ounces sweet white sorghum flour (about 1/3 cup)
  • 1.4 ounces tapioca flour (about 1/3 cup)
  • 1.7 ounces potato starch (about 1/3 cup)
  • .9 ounce flaxseed meal (about 1/4 cup)
  • 1 teaspoon xanthan gum
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 2 large egg whites
  • 1 large egg
  • ¾ cup lower-sodium marinara sauce (such as Dell'Amore)
  • 4 ounces part-skim mozzarella cheese, shredded (about 1 cup)
  • 2 ounces sliced turkey pepperoni
  • 2 tablespoons grated fresh Parmesan cheese
